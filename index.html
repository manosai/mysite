My Projects

TrollSpice - PennApps Fall 2012


TrollSpice was a hardware hack for PennApps Fall 2012 that won Best Life Sciences/Healthcare App. Using Arduino, we hooked up sensors to the human body to interpret muscle contraction as input for various software programs. The aim was to demonstrate the potential for alternative input to be used for physical therapy, communication with the disabled, or to provide deeper insight into personal fitness. Built over the course of 48 hours.
